Dungeon Crawls

Overcome each dungeon’s challenges and confront the boss waiting deep within. Sometimes you can only move forward after hitting a switch or defeating all the monsters in a specific area.

Inaccessible Areas

If a dungeon entrance is blocked off, try returning there after advancing the story.

Don’t Forget to Save

A defeat will send you right back to Rigbarth, so make sure to save at the dungeon’s entrance and before boss fights!

Bring Your Friends

Talk to the townsfolk to bring them on adventures. More companions can help make your fights easier.

BTW Conversations

Press the L Button or R Button while a dialogue screen is displayed to bring up a list of options, including the choice to invite someone on an adventure. However, they may refuse your offer if your friendship level is too low.

Equipping Party Members

Hold an item in your hands and give it to a party member with the A Button to make them equip it. (Keep in mind that they won’t return these items later.) Check their current equipment under the Party page in the Camp Menu.
