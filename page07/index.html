Ranger Work

SEED receives all sorts of requests and missions related to helping the townsfolk and investigating dungeons. Livia, the field captain of Rigbarth Outpost, can also assign you monster-hunting missions.

Directives & SEED Points

Complete requests, ship items, defeat monsters, and talk with the townsfolk to accumulate SEED points that you can use to issue Directives. Speak with Saint Eliza in Rigbarth Outpost to see the current list of Directives. Among other things, Directives let you organize festivals, expand the size of your bag, and equip more rune abilities.

Difficulty Settings

Speak with Saint Eliza to change the game’s difficulty level to Easy, Normal, or Hard at any time.

Wanted Monsters

SEED HQ issues bounties for monsters that have caused lots of trouble in a particular region. Look at your Requests on the Ranger Notes page to review information about a wanted monster.

Ranks & Crests

Livia will give you information about wanted monster missions, which you can complete to improve your SEED rank. Round up specific monsters and rank up to earn SEED Crests. Equip these crests in the Ranger Notes page of the Camp Menu to activate their effects.
