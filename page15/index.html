＊ Rune Factory 5 ＊Map of Rigbarth

True Strike Smithy
Sells equipment.
Martin and Cecil’s House
The two brothers live here.
Serendipity General Store
Sells seeds, produce, and cooking supplies.
The Blue Moon Inn
Visit its hot springs to restore your HP/RP and wash away your fatigue.
Studio Palmo
Sells furniture and building expansions.
The Crystalabra
Trade for crystals and buy both magic spells and rune abilities here.
Farm Dragon
Travel to the fields located on the backs of your farm dragons.
Rigbarth Outpost
Livia, Scarlett, and Saint Eliza live on the ground floor, while the protagonist lives on the second floor.
The Sweet Hearth
Sells recipe bread. Will also sell clothes after undergoing an expansion.
Lackadaisy
Sells recipe bread for takeout and various dishes for dine-in.
Rigbarth Clinic
Sells medicine. Expand it to buy vaccines that nullify status effects and reduce elemental damage.
Elsje’s House
Elsje lives here with her younger sister, Priscilla.
Great Tree Plaza
This enormous tree has served as the town’s symbol for generations. Events occasionally take place here.
Flowerstruck
Sells flowers, flower seeds, and nutrients.
Terry’s Detective Agency
Check how many treasure chests you’ve opened and accept requests here.
Lucas’ House
Lucas lives here.
Professor Monster & The Renamer
Speak to these residents to change the names of the protagonist and your farm dragons, as well as check the stats of your monster friends.
Belpha Ruins
A sprawling dungeon complex lies beneath these crumbling ruins.
Festival Grounds
Festivals often take place in this large cobblestone plaza.
Abandoned House
Nobody lives here anymore.
