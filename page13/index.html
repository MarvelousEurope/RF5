Befriending the Townsfolk

Increase your friendship levels to invite people on adventures and see special story events. Just talking to someone once a day will help improve your relationship. The Friendship Lv page in the Camp Menu will show if you’re getting along.

Presents

Hand out gifts to raise friendship levels. Giving someone their favorite thing will make them especially happy, so pay attention to everybody’s likes and dislikes!

Birthdays

When someone tells you their birthday, your calendar will update with a character icon for that date.

Expanding Buildings

Visit Studio Palmo to expand your room or one of Rigbarth’s businesses. Doing so will increase the number of rooms and items available to you and the townsfolk. You can also change your room’s interior here.

Festivals

Participate in one of Rigbarth’s many festivals to earn prizes and SEED points. Check your calendar for details such as the festival’s time and location.
