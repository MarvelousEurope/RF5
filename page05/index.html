Field Screen

HP Meter
Displays your current HP (Hit Points). If this hits zero, you’ll faint and automatically wake up at Rigbarth Clinic.
RP Meter
Displays your current RP (Rune Points). Actions such as working with tools, creating items, and using magic or rune abilities all consume RP. If your RP hits zero, you’ll start consuming HP instead.
Party HP Meters
Displays the current HP of each party member. If this hits zero, they’ll fall unconscious.
Information
Displays the names of people, monsters, locations, and objects.
Date and Day
Displays the date and day of the week.
Season
Displays the current season.
Time
Displays the time. The date advances at midnight.
Weather
Displays the current weather.
  • Sunny
  • Cloudy
  • Rain
  • Snow
  • Typhoon
  • Blizzard
  • Starfall 
  • Runeys
  • Night (Clear/Cloudy)
Funds
Displays the amount of gold you have.
Mini-Map
Displays your current location and the surrounding areas. Use the ZR Button to change the size of the mini-map.
Calendar Icon
Tap the icon or press the L Button and − Button simultaneously to open.
Map Icon
Tap the icon or use the − Button to open.
Equipment
Displays the farm tool and weapon you have equipped. Use the Left/Right Buttons to switch between them.
Rune Abilities/Magic
Displays the rune abilities and magic you have equipped.
Weapon/Tool
Displays the weapon or farm tool you have equipped.

Using the Map

Bring up the map with the − Button to see building and dungeon locations, as well as who is in what building. You can warp to previously visited locations with the Y Button. Select the diamond-shaped icons to warp around Rigbarth itself.

Press X to Go Home

Press the X Button on the map screen to warp from your current location to your room.

Camp Menu Screen

Press the + Button to open the Camp Menu and check your stats, items, requests, and game settings.

Status
Displays your stats and current amount of lumber/material stone. Also lets you check the L Pocket.
Bag
See your items, change your equipment, set rune abilities, and customize the L Pocket.
Skills
Displays your skill levels for different weapons, elements, and actions.
Recipes
Displays the recipes you can use to make food, equipment, and other items.
Party
Displays the current status of any party members or monster companions.
Friendship Lv
Displays the level of friendship you’ve achieved with the townsfolk and any tamed monsters.
Ranger Notes
Displays your requests and the next story-related objective, as well as any SEED crests or stamps you’ve collected.
Save/Load
Save your progress at a save point or load another file.
Options
Adjust the game’s volume, system settings, and controls.

L Pocket
Using the L Pocket

Press the L Button on the field screen to open a shortcut known as the L Pocket. Customize the L Pocket on the Bag page to register weapons, farm tools, accessories, rune abilities, food, and various other items for easy access.
