Field Controls

Left Stick
Run/Walk (while holding R Button)
Directional Buttons
Switch between farm tools (Left) and weapons (Right)
A Button
Talk/Check/Pick up (hold down to gather items of the same type)
B Button
Perform action
X Button/Y Button
Use rune abilities or magic
L Button
Open L Pocket/Begin a BTW conversation
R Button
Dash/Begin a BTW conversation
ZL Button
Throw spell seal/Connect with monster (press and hold)
ZR Button
Expand/Shrink the mini-map
+ Button
Open Camp Menu
− Button
Open map
Right Stick
Control camera
Right Stick (press down)
Reset camera/Lock on
L Button & − Button (press simultaneously)
Open calendar

Charge attack

Link attack (when character icon is flashing)

B Button Pressed Repeatedly at End of Combo
