Your Room

Visit your room on the second floor of Rigbarth Outpost to save the game, organize your furniture, and view past cutscenes with the projector. Sleeping in your bed will restore your HP/RP and automatically advance the time to 6 AM the next morning.

Placing Furniture

Furniture you’ve bought from Studio Palmo will appear on a platform in your room. Interact with a piece of furniture and select “Pick it up” from the menu to move it wherever you like, using the furniture outline as a guide. If this outline turns red, then you can’t place anything there.

Furniture Storage Area

You can send any furniture you’ve bought to your room or a storage area on a farm dragon. However, you can’t buy new furniture until you’ve moved your previous purchase. Placing any furniture on an empty platform will also prevent you from buying new furniture, so keep the area clear!

Licenses & Equipment

You need special equipment, such as a forge or crafting table, and the appropriate license to create new items. Talk to Saint Eliza to issue a Directive that lets you take a license test, then buy the equipment you need to get started.

Combining Materials

Create new items by combining ingredients and materials according to a recipe. You can also add other things to put your own twist on it.

Recipe Bread

Buy recipe bread at Lackadaisy or the Sweet Hearth to learn new recipes for anything from weapons and tools to medicines and accessories. Each category has its own kind of bread, so make sure you eat the right one.
